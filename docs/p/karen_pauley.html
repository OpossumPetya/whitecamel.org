Karen Pauley

Award received in 2016.

For years, Karen Pauley has been on the shortlist of people nominated for a White Camel Award for her work as the president of The Perl Foundation. For several years she has kept the gears turning, oversaw various major improvements in management, and putting everything in good order. This year she has stepped out of her role in TP. Since she's no longer part of TPF, we can recognize her with this award. Mark Keating has a much longer discussion of her work at TPF.
