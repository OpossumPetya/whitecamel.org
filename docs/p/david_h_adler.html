David H. Adler

Award received in 2001.

David H. Adler is a founding member of the first Perl user group, the New York Perl Mongers. Later he became the head of the group and still keeps the group going. He was instrumental in forming and developing other Perl user groups across the world and is active on many of their mailing lists.
