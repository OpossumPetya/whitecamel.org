Chris Nandor is one of the leading advocates of Perl.  Besides his regular
Perl News column available on the Web, in email, and in the print and
Web editions of The Perl Journal and maintaining the community
discussion site <a href="http://use.perl.org">use.perl.org</a>, he is
the leading advocate of MacPerl, ported by Matthias Neeracher. Chris is
the author of the perlport manual page which gives Perl developers a
guide to writing portable code.
