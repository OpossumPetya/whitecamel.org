We are pleased to announce Mohammad Sajid Anwar (<a href="https://metacpan.org/author/MANWAR">MANWAR</a>) as the White Camel Award 2022 recipient.
Mohammad runs the <a href="https://theweeklychallenge.org/">Perl weekly challenge</a>, a coding competition where a group of Perl enthusiasts, from beginners to experts, come together and solve 2 tasks every week. So far the challenge has attracted more than 300 unique participants.

Furthermore, Mohammad is one of two active editors of the <a href="https://perlweekly.com/">Perl Weekly Newsletter</a> where he discusses topics such as toy algorithms and fun simple games.
