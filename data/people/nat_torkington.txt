Nat Torkington has been involved in many areas of Perl besides his many
technical contributions.  He's a major contributor to the core Perl
documentation and the Perl FAQ, senior editor of <i><a
href="http://www.tpj.com">The Perl Journal</a></i>, moderator of the
Perl advocacy mailing list (among others), and has been the program
chair for The Perl Conference in 1999 and 2000. Nat is the coauthor of
The Perl Cookbook  from O'Reilly &amp; Associates.
